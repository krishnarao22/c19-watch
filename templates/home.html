How can we defeat the unseen enemy?

You can save a life.

Social Distancing.

Social Distancing helps reduce the number of people who get infected. It also helps 'flatten the curve', reducing the strain on our healthcare system.

Wash Your Hands.

Washing your hands often will helps stop the virus from getting to you and those you care about. Be sure to wash your hands before eating especially!

Cover up.

Cover your face with a mask (or at least a cloth) when around others! Remember to cover coughs and sneezes using the inside of your elbow or a tissue. This isn't a substitute for social distancing though!
